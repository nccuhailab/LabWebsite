Our Mission

We design, develop, and disseminate knowledge of human-automation interaction technologies that assist and collaborate with people. We focus on enhancing collaboration and synergy between humans and advanced technologies through interdisciplinary research.

Our Team

We are an interdisciplinary team affiliated with the Department of Management Information Systems at National Chengchi University. Our lab brings together researchers passionate about understanding and improving human interaction with automated systems and robots.

Meet Our Team

Our Research

Our research spans three key domains: Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), Electroencephalography (EEG) for cognitive assessment, and Human-Automation Interaction (HAI). We conduct both basic and applied research to investigate the design of embodied AI systems that work effectively with people.

Explore Our Research

Our Publications

We are committed to disseminating our research findings through peer-reviewed publications and open science practices. Our work has been published in leading conferences and journals in HCI, robotics, and human factors.
